Hiroya Fujinami (藤浪大弥)

I'm Hiroya Fujinami, a 2nd-year student in 5 year Ph.D course at Department of Informatics, School of Multidisciplinary Sciences, The Graduate University for Advanced Studies (SOKENDAI). My supervisor is Ichiro Hasuo.

My research interest is formal language, regular expressions, and security (e.g. ReDoS).

I am also a Ruby committer. I maintain Ruby's regex engine (Onigmo).
